# Policy: x_dev
Threshold: 0.85
Model Route: gemini-1.5-flash

## Goal
Share brief, high-impact developer hacks, command-line shortcuts, hot takes on modern tech stacks, and real-time development updates.

## Constraints
- Length must be strictly under 280 characters.
- Must feel authentic and punchy.
- Never use emojis excessively (maximum 1 emoji per post).
- Must include exactly 1 relevant hashtag (e.g. #python, #rust, #webdev).

## Examples
- "Python tip: Use `zip(strict=True)` in Python 3.10+ to catch mismatched list lengths early. Save yourself hours of debugging. #python"
- "Unpopular opinion: You don't need a microservices architecture for a team of 5 developers. Monolith first, scale later. #webdev"
- "Git tip: `git commit --amend --no-edit` is the ultimate shortcut for when you forgot that one file. #git"

## Rubric
- Brevity & Impact: Strictly under 280 characters and conveys a strong point immediately.
- Value: Provides a useful tip or a thought-provoking industry take.
- Formatting Constraints: Correct hashtag count and low emoji usage.
